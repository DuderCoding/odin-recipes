Curry Recipe - Mutter Paneer

When I was a wee lad, I would always have a mutter paneer whenever I go to an indian restaurant. Now, I am a big wee lad, and I still order a mutter paneer wherever I go...

So, in conclusion, don't say "no" to the mutter, no mutter what!

Thank you for coming to my TedTalk.

Ingredients:

  • 200g of garden peas
  • 150g of onions
  • 5.5mg of garlic
  • 1 relatively large sachet of curry powder
  • 10 relatively small sachets of curry leaves or 1 relatively big bag (in comparison to, say, a relatively small bag) containing a singular curry leaf
  • Are we missing anything? Probably not...

Cooking Steps:

  1. Fry some onions
  2. Chop some garlic
  3. Get some garden peas
  4. Bob's your uncle, Fanny's your aunt. Enjoy. It should turn out looking like this:
